Systems Solutions: Pharma & Biotech

High-purity extraction, decolorization, and purification solutions engineered to meet stringent pharmaceutical processing standards.

Biochemical Processing Expertise

The purification of pharmaceutical and biotechnical compounds demands specialized, highly porous resin matrices that won't denature sensitive molecules. We provide FDA-compliant and highly regulated resins tailored to life sciences.

Our Macroporous adsorption resins act through Van der Waals forces to successfully separate and refine active pharmaceutical ingredients (APIs), extract antibiotics from fermentation broths, purify plant extracts, and selectively remove toxic proteins or colored bodies.
